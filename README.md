# FeedTheFuture

**FeedTheFuture** is a reusable software application developed as part of the **Software Reuse** course (Winter Semester 2024–25) at **Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT)**. The project was created under the theme **"Sustainable Development Goals in India"** and aligns with **SDG 2 – Zero Hunger**, which aims to end hunger, achieve food security, improve nutrition, and promote sustainable agriculture.

The primary goal of FeedTheFuture is to support the vision of **Zero Hunger** by highlighting the efforts of people who help those in need. The application introduces the "other side" of society by showcasing the real heroes—the donors whose helping hands reach people experiencing hunger. By recognizing these acts of kindness, the platform encourages more individuals to participate in reducing hunger and making a positive social impact.

The application is designed with software reusability in mind, allowing its components to be reused and extended for similar social-impact applications. FeedTheFuture focuses on spreading awareness, inspiring community participation, and supporting the Sustainable Development Goals through technology.

This project was developed as part of the SDG initiative, and the collection of student projects is hosted on **http://sdgnexus.in/**.
